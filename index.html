Potatoes

April 1, 2020 by website owner

In recent years, potatoes have been the world's fourth-largest food crop after corn, wheat, and rice. Such a huge production of potatoes is just proof that growing potatoes is not difficult at all! But if you want to produce the amount of potatoes you consume in one year, you need to prepare a bit wider space in your garden!
You should also know that the vegetative and fruiting parts of the potato contain the toxin solanine which is dangerous for human consumption. Fortunately, we do not eat the leaves and fruits of potatoes, but the tuber:))


Some basics...



Potato Seeds


Potato seed is the potato itself! When used as a seed, potatoes should be smaller than a golf ball. In case they are bigger, cut them in a half carefully, to avoid damaging the eyes. If you have no previous experience in potato planting, I suggest some in the usual variety for starters.
